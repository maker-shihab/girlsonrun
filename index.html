Volunteer

The impact Girls on the Run makes in your local community is dependent on dedicated volunteers like you, who are committed and passionate about our mission and vision.

Not a runner?
Not a problem!

Become a Coach

Coaches are the heart of our program! Each season, two to three coaches inspire and guide a team of participants through programming during a time when they need it most. If you’re ready to directly make an impact, then this role is for you.

Learn More Coach Registration

Become a junior Coach

Junior Coaches are needed to inspire the next generation! Our Junior Coach program is for high school girls aged 16 to 18 years old. Beyond serving as a role model to program participants, Junior Coaches work alongside Coaches and gain a sense of belonging, leadership and impact.

Additional Volunteer Opportunities

We have a role for everyone!

5K Volunteers

Our end of season 5K provides a tangible sense of accomplishment to participants. To celebrate and make the experience one to remember, there are many opportunities to get involved.

  • Registration
  • Medal Station
  • Water Station
  • Happy Hair Stations
  • Running Buddy opportunities, where we invite family members and/or trusted friends to run alongside participants.
Learn More

Group Opportunities

Are you part of a group or organization that wants to make an impact on our community? Girls on the Run would love to have you get involved with our program. Here are few ways you can make a difference while working together.

  • Volunteer together at a Girl on the Run 5K event – from cheer teams to water stations and more – there are endless opportunities to celebrate girls through accomplishment!
  • Support with one-day volunteer opportunities. Volunteer for or financially support a team for a season.
